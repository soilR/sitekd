For a better overview, I built groups of the commonly employed methods to characterize the soil redox status:

  • EH measurements by redox electrodes
  • Dyes that indicate the presence of, e.g., Fe2+
  • IRIS tools (Indicator of Reduction In Soils) that indicate Mn and Fe reducing conditions by dissolution of synthetic minerals
  • Others (e.g.)
    • Iron metal rods
